Kuo et al 2010 CgAp-1 Reanalysis

Bin He

Goal

Reanalyze Yap1 ChIP in C. glabrata in order to identify the genomic regions bound by the TF.

Reference 1. Kuo D, Licon K, Bandyopadhyay S, Chuang R, Luo C, Catalana J, et al. Coevolution within a transcriptional network by compensatory trans and cis mutations. Genome Res. 2010 Dec 1;20(12):1672–8.

Data Three replicates for CgAp-1 ChIP. For each replicate, the IP enriched DNA is labeled with Cy5 (and the corresponding unenriched (input) DNA is labeled with Cy3. The mixture of the two was hybridized to a custom Agilent microarray, scanned using a Genepix 4000A scanner and analyzed by GenePix 6.0, producing a two-color array dataset in the gpr format.
